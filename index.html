Bittersweet

Raleigh, NC
bittersweetraleigh.com



Nestled in downtown Raleigh, Bittersweet offers a truly unique experience with their menu of seasonal and classic cocktails alongside fantastic desserts. As they say, they are a place where you can get bourbon with your pie, champagne with your cake and Irish whiskey in your coffee.
Alongside their alcohol and dessert choices, they have a full coffee and espresso menu. Desserts are baked in-house daily, and they offer over 40 types of gin. If you're looking to relax and forget a hard days work, be sure to look for their Mind Eraser- a coffee liquer and vodka shooter.

Lucky 32 Southern Kitchen

Cary, NC
lucky32.com


The original Lucky 32 was opened in 1989 in Greensboro, this location opened in 2002. This wonderful restaurant prides themselves on putting the guests and it's employees first and foremost. Their attention to detail doesn't stop just the decor and service, but certainly can be noted in their food as well.
One of the most unique aspects, is the ability to go to their website and look directly at their recipes! Whether it's the Smoked Ham & Cheese Dip you enjoyed or the Smoked Salmon Cakes, you'll find the recipes online and available with no catch. However, don't let that stop you from going in person and enjoying a night out.

Brewery Bhavana

Raleigh, NC
https://brewerybhavana.com/



Another gem in downtown Raleigh right near their sister restaurant, Bida Manda, is this one. A unique combination of a bar, restaurant, flower shop, and bookstore. That's right, it is all those things in one and somehow beautifully done.
The dim sum here is incredible. There's no arguing it's literal translation of "touch the heart" when you bite into a warm dim sum and you just feel the warmth of the food and feel home. Don't forget to try a beer while you're there as well. After all, it is called Brewery Bhavana for a reason.
